Welcome to ResumeApp

On left side you can select what you want to do

There is option for Profile settings where you can edit your email and password.

In Personal details you can set gender, driving licence and address.

Education is for your finished schools and courses.

With skills you can show how you can handle things what you learned or using every day.

Experience is something where you can show your positions where did you work or still working.

In additional info you can write some basic information about you like hobbies or what you like.

Resume part is where you can find your resume what is created from your whole information what you set in every part of application.
